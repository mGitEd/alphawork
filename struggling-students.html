The Core Insight

Students are practicing extensively but not testing. 100% meet XP targets (avg 427%!) but only 30% have mastered their effective grade. The intervention needed is clear: shift focus from practice time to testing frequency and pass rates at 90%+. More practice without validation isn't producing growth.
