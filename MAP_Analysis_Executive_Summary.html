Executive Summary

Bottom Line: The program is performing well overall (3.56x average growth vs 2x goal), but there are significant opportunities to improve outcomes through better app difficulty calibration, optimizing time allocation, and targeted interventions for struggling students.

Key Win: EGUMPP Impact

EGUMPP users show 75% higher growth than non-users (4.81x vs 2.75x). This is the strongest app-growth correlation in the data.

Critical Finding: Time Paradox

Students spending MORE time are growing LESS. Q1 (1,534 min): 4.59x growth vs Q4 (3,687 min): 2.81x growth. Quality over quantity matters.

Concern: Language App Calibration

40.9% of students earn >2 XP/min in Language apps - content may be too easy or students are gaming. Need difficulty recalibration.

Opportunity: Vocabulary Focus

Vocab-heavy students (>65% time) achieve 4.67x growth vs Language-heavy (<35%): 2.66x. Increasing vocab time could boost outcomes.

Actionable Recommendations

  • HIGH
    Recalibrate Language App Difficulty

    40.9% of students earn >2 XP/min, indicating content is too easy or gaming. Increase difficulty levels in MobyMax and Freckle. Implement anti-gaming measures.

  • HIGH
    Expand EGUMPP Usage

    EGUMPP shows 75% higher growth for users. Currently limited to 7th grade base course - consider expanding to Middle School and High School broadly. Users: 39% vs potential 100%.

  • HIGH
    Rebalance Time for Language-Heavy Students

    94 students with <35% vocab time average only 2.66x growth. Shift 15-20% of their language time to vocabulary instruction.

  • MEDIUM
    Quality Over Quantity Intervention

    Highest-time students (Q4) show lowest growth. Review students spending 3,000+ minutes - they may need different interventions, not more time.

  • MEDIUM
    Level 1 (K-3) Support Program

    Youngest students show lowest average growth (3.06x). Develop age-appropriate engagement strategies and review content difficulty calibration for this group.

  • MEDIUM
    Vocabloco Engagement Quality Check

    Overall Vocabloco usage doesn't predict success, but high XP earners do well. Focus on meaningful engagement metrics, not just app usage.

  • LOW
    Study Alpha High School Practices

    89.3% meeting goal (highest) with 5.50x average growth. Document and share their instructional practices across other campuses.

  • LOW
    New Student Onboarding Focus

    0-3 month students show 2.77x growth vs 4.48x for 7-12 month students. Improve initial onboarding and set realistic early expectations.
